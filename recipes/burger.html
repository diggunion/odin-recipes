Basic Burger

A Tasty Homemade Burger

Bring a restaurant quality dish to your dinner table, and discover how easy it is to satisfy your taste buds.

This classic beef patty can be dressed up in a variety of different ways.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp evoo
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • ½ tsp basil
  • 4 buns
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • (opt) Chopped lettuce, onion, tomato, pickles

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to 450°F.
  2. Mix together ground beef, garlic, evoo, salt, pepper, and basil.
  3. Divide into 4 balls and flatten into patties.
  4. Cook the patties for 3-5 minutes each side.
  5. Remove patties from grill and place onto buns. Dress with desired toppings and condiments.
